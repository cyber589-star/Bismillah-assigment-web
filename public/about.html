Who We Are

Mehtab Inam is a premier platform in Pakistan connecting talented individuals with professional assignment and typing work. We provide high-quality handwritten and MS Word document solutions for students, professionals, and organizations.

Founded on the principles of integrity and excellence, we offer daily salary payouts via JazzCash and EasyPaisa, ensuring our workers are compensated fairly and on time. Our platform has empowered hundreds of individuals across Pakistan to earn a dignified income from home.

Whether it's assignments, projects, PDF scanning, or handwritten work, we deliver exceptional quality that meets the highest academic and professional standards.
